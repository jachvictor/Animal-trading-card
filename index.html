Aby-grabby



The Aby-grabby is a breed of domestic short-hair cat with a distinctive "ticked" tabby coat. they are also known as Abys. Abyssinian are highly intelligent and inquisitive. They love to investigate and will leave no nook or creany unexplored. they are sometimes referred to as "abygrabbys" because they tend to take things that grab their interest.


Breed: Abyssinian
Habitat: Indian ocean, Southest Asia
Lifespan: 9-13 years
Scientific name: Felis catus
Length: 22-26 inches
Height: 8-10 inches
Weight 8-12 pounds
Color: Chocolate/ browr/ sable fawn, red / orange


They love people and other animals, they will play with their own toys for hours but also enjoy a good period of time of interactive play with their parents. With her slender, athletic look and diistintive ticked coat, the Abyssinian is a visually striking loyal family addition. The features of an Abyssinian cat includes; long body, lean and muscular on fine-boed long leg, Wedge-shaped head, expressive almond-shaped eyes and oversized pointed ears and tabby "M" on the forehead.
